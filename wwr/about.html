The Great Water Rapids

Welcome to The Great Water Rapids! Our mission is to provide thrilling, safe, and unforgettable water adventures. We believe in the power of nature to inspire and bring people together.

History

Founded in 1985, The Great Water Rapids started with a single raft and a passion for river adventures. Over the years, we have grown into a full-service rafting company with multiple guides and tours.

We continue to grow as a company and offer more tours to a wider range of rafting enthusiasts. Our team is always ready to bring you the best rafting experience.
